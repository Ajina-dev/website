Baby Olive

Exclusive Premium Baby Shop

Baby Olive, was started in 2021 by a couple who had just set off on their journey into parenthood. Their objective was to make available all the items needed for a baby under one roof along with a different shopping ambience. Unlike the regular baby stores, the interiors of the shops are one of a kind. Each and every corner was specifically designed in the Bohemian theme. Baby Olive has regular customers online and offline and will be expanding by giving franchise shops. They aim to be a one of a kind clothing store pan India. The two branches are in Attingal & Kochi (Oberon Mall). We care about the health and proper development of your child
